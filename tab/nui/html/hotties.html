Hotkeys & Commands

Server hotkeys and commands, this isn't all of them but its a good enough list


[RP Animations]
Z - To Open Your Animation Wheel
B - To Point
X - Handsup
K - Ragdoll
LCTRL - Crouch
, - Surrender

[Car Controls]
Z - To Open Your Car Controls
PGUP - To Increase Your Cruise Control Speed
PGDOWN - To Decrease Your Cruise Control Speed

[Interactions]
~ - To Open Your Phone
F1 - To Open Your Interaction Menu
F2 - To Open Your Inventory
F6 - To Open Your Job Menu
F9 - To Open Your CAD (LSPD Only)
INS - To Open Vmenu (Limited Options To Players For RP)
Left Shift + H - To Change Voice Distance
G - Rob (Only works when someone is surrendered and you have a weapon)
Left Shift + X - Handcuff (LSPD Only)

[Chat]
T - To Chat
/ooc - To chat out of character - [KEEP THIS SERVER RELATED ONLY PLEASE]
/help - For Questions Or Help Server Related - [IF YOU REQUIRE HELP FROM A STAFF MEMBER]
/anon - To Talk In Chat As Anonymous - [ONLY FOR HOSTILE SITUATIONS]
/advert - To Advertise In Chat
/me

[Commands]
/ui To Hide/Show Health/Armour/Food/Water Status
/gauges To Hide/Show The Gauges When In A Vehicle
